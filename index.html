Cyber Security Club

The Cyber Security Club at the College of Computing and Information Technology, Shaqra University, is a dynamic community dedicated to fostering knowledge and skills in the ever-evolving field of cybersecurity. Through collaborative learning, workshops, and events, the club aims to empower students with practical insights and tools to navigate the digital landscape securely. Join us in our mission to build a strong foundation for cyber awareness and resilience.

  • The club is dedicated for spreading cyber security awareness in the society.
  • Run by a group of motivated students who are trying to spread the cyber awareness around.
  • Supported by College of Computing and Information Technology at Shaqra University

Exciting news! The Cyber Security Club at the College of Computing and Information Technology, Shaqra University, is hosting a conference. This event promises insightful sessions, expert speakers, and opportunities for networking. Stay tuned for updates on this valuable opportunity to delve into the latest trends and challenges in the field of cybersecurity.

Benefits for Attending the Conference sessions.

The Shaqra University Student Cyber Security Conference 2023 is hosted by the students of the Cyber Security Club to provide answers for your queries in Cyber Security along with spreading the awareness. The conference enables multiple benefits for you as:

  • Knowledge Expansion: Gain insights into the latest trends, threats, and technologies in cybersecurity through keynote speakers, workshops, and presentations. .
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with industry professionals, experts, and peers. Networking can lead to collaborations, job opportunities, and a broader understanding of the cybersecurity community. .

Conference Tracks

Events at the Conference

Expert Sessions

Attending expert speaker sessions on cybersecurity offers valuable insights, latest trends, and practical knowledge. It helps stay informed about evolving threats, best practices, and strategies to enhance your understanding and skills in safeguarding digital assets.

Students Case Studies

Hearing students' case studies in cybersecurity provides real-world examples, diverse perspectives, and innovative solutions. It offers a chance to understand practical challenges, learn from others' experiences, and gain insights into emerging issues from the perspective of those entering the field.

Cyber Security Threats

Learning about cybersecurity threats is crucial for understanding potential risks and vulnerabilities in digital systems. It allows individuals and organizations to proactively identify, prevent, and respond to security incidents, enhancing overall cybersecurity posture and protecting against potential cyber attacks.

Live Demos

Watching live demos on cybersecurity provides a dynamic and practical way to see how various attacks unfold and how security measures can be applied in real-time. It enhances understanding, helps identify vulnerabilities, and enables hands-on learning, preparing individuals to respond effectively to potential cyber threats.

Cyber Security Safety Measures

Cybersecurity is a critical aspect of our digital lives, as our online activities grow more extensive and interconnected. To ensure our safety online, it is imperative to implement various cybersecurity measures.

Open Forum Q&A

Open Forum Q&A is an interactive platform that brings people together to exchange knowledge, ideas, and perspectives. It offers a space for individuals to ask questions and receive answers from a diverse community, allowing for a rich and dynamic discussion.

General Queries

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How will the conference help me ?

    Conferences provide access to a wealth of knowledge and expertise in the cybersecurity field. You can attend presentations, workshops, and training sessions conducted by renowned professionals and researchers. These sessions can deepen your understanding of cybersecurity concepts, introduce you to new technologies and methodologies, and expose you to real-world case studies. The insights gained from these educational opportunities can supplement your academic studies and enhance your overall knowledge base.

  • What is the time and venue for the conference?

    Conference will be organized by the College of Computing and Information Technology Shaqra University at the main auditorium. This conference is in collaboration with the Deanship of Students Affairs Shaqra University. Date of the conference is 6 December 2023 from morning 10:00 AM

  • Is there any registration Charges for the Conference?

    The conference is FREE OF COST and does not hold any charges towards registration. You can connect back to the technical team for the registration directly.

  • What will happen in the Conference?

    Cybersecurity conferences typically feature a range of activities and events designed to educate, engage, and connect professionals in the field. Conferences often kick off with keynote presentations by industry leaders, renowned experts, or influential figures in cybersecurity.

  • What is the take home delight after the conference is over?

    The conference comes with the bunch of knowledge items that you earn during the sessions. The acquired knowledge is the by product of the conference. However, the CERTIFICATE of PARTIICPATION is also available for all registered participants.

  • Is there any live demonstrations to learn?

    There will be couple of presentations to reflect the real time Cyber Security hazards and awareness.
